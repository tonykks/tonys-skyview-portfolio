A

Logic of Be Verbs

Rule Subject-Verb Agreement (짝꿍 찾기)

주어가 누구냐에 따라 Be동사의 옷(형태)이 바뀝니다.

Subject (주어) Present (현재) Past (과거)
I (1인칭/나) am was
You (2인칭/너) are were
He / She / It (3인칭 단수) is was
We / You / They (복수) are were

Be동사는 뒤에 무엇이 오느냐에 따라 해석이 완전히 달라집니다.

1. Linking Verb (연결 동사)

Subject = Complement

해석: ~이다 (Identity/State)

Pattern: Be + Noun (명사)

She is a teacher.

(그녀는 선생님이다.)

Pattern: Be + Adjective (형용사)

They are happy.

(그들은 행복한 상태이다.)

2. Existence (존재)

Subject exists at Location

해석: ~있다 (Location)

Pattern: Be + Preposition (전치사)

The book is on the desk.

(그 책은 책상 위에 있다.)

Pattern: Be + Preposition Phrase

I am in my room.

(나는 내 방 안에 있다.)

B

Logic of General Verbs

Rule 3rd Person Singular (3인칭 단수)

주어가 나(I)도 아니고 너(You)도 아닌 제3자 하나일 때,
현재형 동사 뒤에 -s 또는 -es를 붙입니다.
※ 중요: He/She/It 뿐만 아니라 [Tom, My Mom, The Desk, A Cat] 등 모든 "하나의 명사"가 3인칭 단수입니다!

1st Person

I like pizza.

2nd Person

You like pizza.

3rd Person

My brother likes pizza.

Special!

일반동사의 핵심 구분 기준은 "진행형(V-ing)을 만들 수 있는가?" 입니다.

1. Action Verbs (동작동사)

Progressive OK (O)

시작과 끝이 있는 움직임입니다. (진행형 가능)

Play play / played

He plays soccer.

(그는 축구를 한다.)

Eat eat / ate

She eats pizza.

(그녀는 피자를 먹는다.)

Study study / studied

I study English.

(나는 영어를 공부한다.)

Run run / ran

It runs fast.

(그것은 빨리 달린다.)

Sleep sleep / slept

The baby sleeps much.

(그 아기는 많이 잔다.)

2. Stative Verbs (상태동사)

Progressive NO (X)

감정, 소유, 인지 등 지속적인 상태입니다. (진행형 불가)

Like like / liked

He likes apples.

(그는 사과를 좋아한다.)

Love love / loved

She loves cats.

(그녀는 고양이를 사랑한다.)

Know know / knew

We know him.

(우리는 그를 안다.)

Have have / had

They have a car.

(그들은 차를 가지고 있다.)

Want want / wanted

She wants water.

(그녀는 물을 원한다.)

A

Be Verb Negative Rules

Rule Subject + Be + NOT ~ (뒤에 붙임)

Present (현재)

  • 1st Sing I am not a student. (나는 학생이 아니다.)
  • 2nd Sing You are not busy. (너는 바쁘지 않다.)
  • 3rd Person Tom is not my brother. (Tom은 내 형제가 아니다.)
  • 3rd Thing The bag is not heavy. (그 가방은 무겁지 않다.)
  • 3rd Animal The dog is not hungry. (그 개는 배고프지 않다.)
  • 1st Plural We are not tired. (우리는 피곤하지 않다.)
  • 2nd Plural You are not late. (너희들은 늦지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Person My friends are not here. (내 친구들은 여기에 없다.)
  • 3rd Thing The cars are not fast. (그 차들은 빠르지 않다.)
  • 3rd Animal Lions are not pets. (사자는 애완동물이 아니다.)

Past (과거)

  • 1st Sing I was not happy then. (나는 그때 행복하지 않았다.)
  • 2nd Sing You were not at home. (너는 집에 없었다.)
  • 3rd Person She was not a doctor. (그녀는 의사가 아니었다.)
  • 3rd Thing The computer was not new. (그 컴퓨터는 새것이 아니었다.)
  • 3rd Animal The cat was not small. (그 고양이는 작지 않았다.)
  • 1st Plural We were not ready. (우리는 준비되지 않았었다.)
  • 2nd Plural You were not loud. (너희들은 시끄럽지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Person They were not students. (그들은 학생이 아니었다.)
  • 3rd Thing Those books were not mine. (저 책들은 내 것이 아니었다.)
  • 3rd Animal Birds were not in the cage. (새들은 새장 안에 없었다.)
B

General Verb Negative Rules

★ 중요 개념: 3인칭 단수란?

He/She/It 뿐만 아니라 나(I)와 너(You)가 아닌 모든 "하나의 명사"
(Tom, The desk, The dog)를 말합니다.

Rule Sub + DO/DOES + NOT + Root (앞에 붙임)

Present (현재)

  • 1st Sing I do not like onions. (나는 양파를 좋아하지 않는다.)
  • 2nd Sing You do not play piano. (너는 피아노를 치지 않는다.)
  • 3rd Person My father does not smoke. (내 아버지는 담배를 피우지 않는다.)
  • 3rd Thing The bus does not stop here. (그 버스는 여기서 멈추지 않는다.)
  • 3rd Animal A rabbit does not eat meat. (토끼는 고기를 먹지 않는다.)
  • 1st Plural We do not want trouble. (우리는 문제를 원하지 않는다.)
  • 2nd Plural You do not know the answer. (너희들은 정답을 모른다.)
  • 3rd Person Teachers do not run in school. (선생님들은 학교에서 뛰지 않는다.)
  • 3rd Thing Computers do not sleep. (컴퓨터는 잠을 자지 않는다.)
  • 3rd Animal Fish do not fly. (물고기는 날지 않는다.)

Past (과거)

  • 1st Sing I did not study yesterday. (나는 어제 공부하지 않았다.)
  • 2nd Sing You did not call me. (너는 나에게 전화하지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Person Tom did not eat breakfast. (Tom은 아침을 먹지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Thing The rain did not stop. (비가 그치지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Animal The dog did not bark. (그 개는 짖지 않았다.)
  • 1st Plural We did not play soccer. (우리는 축구를 하지 않았다.)
  • 2nd Plural You did not lose the game. (너희들은 게임에서 지지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Person My parents did not agree. (내 부모님은 동의하지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Thing Stores did not open. (상점들이 문을 열지 않았다.)
  • 3rd Animal Dinosaurs did not survive. (공룡들은 살아남지 못했다.)

A

Be Verb Question Rules

Rule Be + Subject + ... ? (앞으로 이동)

Present (현재)

  • 1st Sing Am I late? (내가 늦었니?)
  • 2nd Sing Are you okay? (너 괜찮니?)
  • 3rd Person Is Tom your brother? (Tom이 네 형제니?)
  • 3rd Thing Is the bag heavy? (그 가방 무겁니?)
  • 3rd Animal Is the dog hungry? (그 개 배고프니?)
  • 1st Plural Are we ready? (우리 준비됐니?)
  • 2nd Plural Are you students? (너희들은 학생이니?)
  • 3rd Person Are they friends? (그들은 친구니?)

Past (과거)

  • 1st Sing Was I wrong? (내가 틀렸었니?)
  • 2nd Sing Were you at home? (너 집에 있었니?)
  • 3rd Person Was she a doctor? (그녀는 의사였니?)
  • 3rd Thing Was the movie good? (그 영화 좋았니?)
  • 3rd Animal Was the cat small? (그 고양이는 작았니?)
  • 1st Plural Were we loud? (우리가 시끄러웠니?)
  • 3rd Person Were they happy? (그들은 행복했니?)
B

General Verb Question Rules

★ 핵심 주의사항: 3인칭 단수

Does가 앞에 나오면, 뒤에 있는 동사는 반드시 원형(Root)으로 돌아갑니다! (s 빼기)

Rule DO/DOES + Sub + Root ?

Present (현재)

  • 1st Sing Do I look okay? (나 괜찮아 보이니?)
  • 2nd Sing Do you like pizza? (너 피자 좋아하니?)
  • 3rd Person Does he play soccer? (그는 축구를 하니?)
  • 3rd Thing Does it work? (그거 작동하니?)
  • 3rd Animal Does the cat sleep much? (그 고양이는 많이 자니?)
  • 1st Plural Do we need this? (우리 이게 필요하니?)
  • 3rd Person Do they speak English? (그들은 영어를 하니?)

Past (과거)

  • 1st Sing Did I say that? (내가 그렇게 말했니?)
  • 2nd Sing Did you finish? (너 끝냈니?)
  • 3rd Person Did Tom eat lunch? (Tom은 점심 먹었니?)
  • 3rd Thing Did the bus stop? (버스가 멈췄었니?)
  • 3rd Animal Did the dog bark? (그 개가 짖었니?)
  • 1st Plural Did we win? (우리가 이겼니?)
  • 3rd Person Did they agree? (그들이 동의했니?)
