2. Be Verb Examples (Be동사)

규칙: 주어와 Be동사 Swap + 의문사 변환 & 이동

WHO 사람

1 That is Tom.

2 Is that Tom?

3 Is that (Tom)?

4 Is that Who?

5 Who is that?
↺That is Tom.
WHAT 사물

1 This is a camera.

2 Is this a camera?

3 Is this (a camera)?

4 Is this What?

5 What is this?
↺This is a camera.
WHERE 장소

1 They are in the park.

2 Are they in the park?

3 Are they (in the park)?

4 Are they Where?

5 Where are they?
↺They are in the park.
WHEN 시간

1 The party is at 7.

2 Is the party at 7?

3 Is the party (at 7)?

4 Is the party When?

5 When is the party?
↺The party is at 7.
WHY 이유

1 She is sad because it rains.

2 Is she sad because it rains?

3 Is she sad (because it rains)?

4 Is she sad Why?

5 Why is she sad?
↺She is sad because it rains.
HOW 상태

1 You are fine.

2 Are you fine?

3 Are you (fine)?

4 Are you How?

5 How are you?
↺I am fine. (You ↔ I)

3. General Verb Examples (일반동사)

규칙: Do/Does/Did + 주어 + 동사원형 + 의문사 변환 & 이동

WHO 누구를 (목적어)

1 You like Jane.

2 Do you like Jane?

3 Do you like (Jane)?

4 Do you like Who?

5 Who do you like?
↺I like Jane.
WHAT 무엇을

1 You want water.

2 Do you want water?

3 Do you want (water)?

4 Do you want What?

5 What do you want?
↺I want water.
WHERE 어디에

1 You live in Seoul.

2 Do you live in Seoul?

3 Do you live (in Seoul)?

4 Do you live Where?

5 Where do you live?
↺I live in Seoul.
WHEN 언제

1 She starts at 9.

2 Does she start at 9?

3 Does she start (at 9)?

4 Does she start When?

5 When does she start?
↺She starts at 9.
WHY 왜

1 He cries because he is sad.

2 Does he cry because he is sad?

3 Does he cry (because he is sad)?

4 Does he cry Why?

5 Why does he cry?
↺He cries because he is sad.
HOW 방법 (수단)

1 You go by bus.

2 Do you go by bus?

3 Do you go (by bus)?

4 Do you go How?

5 How do you go?
↺I go by bus.
